Pybot/Theodora

Pybot is a program that's supposed to get
you ready for the day and help you out
throughout the day as well. The first
version of Pybot, version 1.0, was just words
of encouragement to get you ready for the day,
now with Pybot 4.0 along the way, you'll be able
to give Pybot commands with your voice. Pybot 4.0
also has great text-to-speech to be able to communicate
with you as well.

The evolution of Pybot has been tremendous
over the years. From being simple text
displayed on a screen with time delays
in between sentences, to personal
assistants with text-to-speech.
I am currently working on Pybot version
4.0, a personal assistant with text-to-speech
and voice recognition. Pybot 4.0 is planned
to be released anytime before September
of 2018. That doesn't mean that it will be
released in that time, it's just planned to.

Pybot version 4.0 was released on August 17 2018.
It has amazing text-to-speech, voice recognition,
and more commands to get you through the day.
Please send us more ideas for future Pybot updates.
Pybot is now called Theodora, named after Empress Theodora
of the Byzantine Empire. Emperor Justinian married
Theodora and they became known as a power couple.
Pybot 4.0 was renamed to Theodora so people could
easily refer to it.
